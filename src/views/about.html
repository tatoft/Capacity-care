About CareConnect

"CareConnect" is our final delivery, a social network integrated into "CapacityCare" that facilitates interaction between caregivers of people with disabilities. The platform offers personalized profiles, access to educational resources, topical support groups, real-time chats and virtual events. This delivery includes all publications and functionality, improving the quality of life of users by providing access to vital information and fostering meaningful connections.
